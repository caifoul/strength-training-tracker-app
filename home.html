Log Workout

Build a session by adding exercises with sets, reps, and weight. Name the session, mark it as a favorite, and save it to your account. Your history stays synced across every device.
⭐

Favorites

Any session you star while logging shows up here. Use it to quickly revisit go-to workouts — upper body days, leg sessions, or any routine worth repeating.
🤖

Coach

Automated workout guide. It looks at what you've done recently, picks what you should do today, and walks you through exercises one at a time — with a "machine taken" swap option if equipment isn't free.
📋

S.G. Workout

Flexible guided sessions. Choose any of your favorited workouts, log each exercise in any order, add exercises mid-session, and review what you've done and missed before finishing.
👤

Profile

Set your main goal, training split, experience level, and hypertrophy targets. The coach uses this info to give you personalized recommendations.
